Drought Analysis and Agricultural Water Needs

Studying Climate Impacts on Irrigation in Mahakali, Nepal

Overview

This project examines how climate-driven droughts affect irrigation and crop production in the Mahakali Irrigation Command Area, Nepal. Using drought indices and crop modeling, it assesses water needs for maize, paddy, and wheat. This work is part of a manuscript in preparation/publication.
